How We Work

Our aim is to create success for our client. We bring a results oriented process that is collaborative, transparent, and tailored to your goals.

Step 1: Discovery

The initial meeting is all about getting to know each other and the scope of the project. We want to understand your goals, challenges, timelines, stakeholders involved, and outline expectations from the project.

Step 2: Planning and Strategy

We take the learnings from our discovery meeting to conduct analysis and identify opportunities for success. We'll develop a strategic plan tailored to your goals, outcomes and budget, and define key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success

Step 3: Process Mapping

Once the strategy is defined and approved, we begin creating a process map for either the entire marketing strategy, or specific aspects of it. We believe it is critical to understand the entire process so we can easily identify inefficiencies and areas for Improvement.

Step 4: Execution

Once the process is mapped, we begin executing the project to bring your vision into reality. During this phase, we'll implement tactics and campaigns to deliver you the highest ROL

Step 5: Measure and Optimize

The final step is to measure results through a data driven approach at a tactical and strategic level. We'll monitor results and optimize on an ongoing basis and adjust strategy accordingly
